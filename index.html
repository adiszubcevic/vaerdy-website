Our Story

Born from the Plant Floor

Vaerdy was born from a frustration shared by four professionals who spent their careers inside cement: the gap between what technology could deliver and what plants were getting was staggering.

Between them, Naveen, Adis, Shankar, and Haijie had 60+ combined years working with cement plants globally. They walked the floors, troubleshot breakdowns at 2 AM, watched knowledge retire with engineers, and saw millions lost to predictable failures.

They met through FLSmidth. But it was over a late-night conversation in 2025 that the idea crystallized: take everything they knew and put it into the hands of every frontline technician, in real time.

"Our goal is simple: fewer surprises, safer work, and better decisions."

That became Vaerdy — from the Danish word for "value." Not a buzzword, but measurable: every euro saved, every hour reclaimed, every life made safer.

Common Questions

How long does it take to go live?▼
Frontline teams can use the mobile app within 8-12 weeks. Full platform with predictive analytics is live within 20-24 weeks. We start with a 1-week discovery.

No. Vaerdy sits alongside your systems. We simplify data capture, validate it, then sync back into SAP/Oracle via bi-directional integration.
What makes this different from a generic CMMS?▼
Vaerdy is built exclusively for cement. Our AI, taxonomy, predictive models, and mobile execution are all cement-specific. We speak cement — from kiln alignment to ball charge optimization.
What ROI can we expect?▼
For a 3,000 TPD plant: €3-5M annual value. Against €100-200k subscription = 10-30x ROI. Payback typically within 6 months.

Yes. Full offline PWA capability. QR scanning, voice input, glove-mode. Syncs automatically when connection returns.
